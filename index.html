How to Play Guy Fighter

Guy Fighter 2018 is greatest fighter game in the world. You can either play as Guy Fieri or as the Well-Adjusted Adult. Your battle goes until one of the fighters is defeated

Each attacker has 4 unique attacks. The more powerful attacks will be less accurate. Use the shield move to help increase your health, but beware of the shield backfiring.

Now, get ready to play Guy Fighter. Will tacky Ed-Hardyness win or will this be the triumph of norm-core? You may select your player. Choose your character wisely
